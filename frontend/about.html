Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Buwana Tours & Travels has been crafting unforgettable experiences for travelers from all over the world. As a proud family-run company, the name 'Buwana' has been synonymous with travel for over 40 years.

Over these decades, we have conducted more than 5000 tours and provided reliable transport services for schools like OSC and CIS for over 30 years. Additionally, we have offered transport solutions for corporate staff including Galadari and OSC staff, combining safety, comfort, and professionalism.

Humble Beginnings

Starting with a single vehicle and a passion for travel, we quickly built trust with local and international clients.

Expanding Fleet

Over the years, our fleet has grown with modern, comfortable vehicles to serve families, groups, and schools. We offer a wide range of vehicles, including vans (9/14-seater) and buses (28-seater), to accommodate tours and transport needs of all sizes.

Award-Winning Service

Recognized for excellence in hospitality and travel services, we continue to deliver memorable journeys.
